# AutoShield AI — Sample Motor Policy Terms

## What is covered
- Loss or damage by accident, fire, theft, riot
- Third-party legal liability (mandatory)
- Personal accident cover for owner-driver: ₹15 lakh

## What is NOT covered
- Wear and tear, mechanical/electrical breakdown
- Driving without a valid license
- Driving under the influence of alcohol/drugs
- Use outside geographical area (India)
- Consequential losses

## Claim process
1. Inform insurer within 24 hours of incident
2. File FIR for theft/major damage
3. Submit damage photos + estimate
4. Surveyor assessment
5. Approval and payout
