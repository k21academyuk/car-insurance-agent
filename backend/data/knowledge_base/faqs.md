# Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I transfer my NCB to a new car?**
A: Yes, NCB is tied to the policyholder, not the vehicle. You can transfer it via an NCB retention letter.

**Q: What is Zero Depreciation cover?**
A: An add-on where the insurer does not deduct depreciation on replaced parts during a claim, giving you full cost recovery.

**Q: My policy expired 2 months ago. Can I renew?**
A: Up to 90 days post-expiry, you can renew but may lose accumulated NCB. Beyond 90 days, the policy is treated as new.

**Q: Are riots and natural disasters covered?**
A: Comprehensive cover includes riots, strikes, floods, and earthquakes. Third-party only does not.
