# IRDAI Motor Insurance Guidelines (Summary)

> Reference document ingested into ChromaDB for RAG.

## Mandatory Coverage
- Third-party liability cover is mandatory under the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988.
- Own Damage cover is optional.

## IDV (Insured Declared Value)
IDV is calculated based on the manufacturer's listed selling price minus depreciation as per IRDAI slab:
- 0–6 months: 5%
- 6 months–1 year: 15%
- 1–2 years: 20%
- 2–3 years: 30%
- 3–4 years: 40%
- 4–5 years: 50%
- 5+ years: mutually agreed

## NCB (No Claim Bonus)
- After 1 claim-free year: 20%
- After 2 years: 25%
- After 3 years: 35%
- After 4 years: 45%
- After 5 years: 50%

## Claim Settlement
- Insurer must acknowledge claim within 7 days
- Surveyor appointed within 24 hours for claims > ₹50,000
- Final settlement within 30 days of submission of all documents
